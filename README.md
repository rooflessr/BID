# BID
Investigation network
### 🔍
This project is an interactive network visualization tracing the flow of millions of dollars in legal fees, government contracts, and documented conflicts of interest within the **LA Alliance for Human Rights v. City of Los Angeles** (Case 2:20-cv-02291).

The map highlights a disturbing timeline: while the City of Los Angeles paid out over **$2.5M** in legal fees to plaintiff counsel (another $1.6M pending!) and **$5.9M** to outside defense firms, the lead plaintiff, **Gary Whitter**, died unclaimed in an alley in March 2025.

### 📍
* **Conflict:** Tracking Elizabeth Mitchell’s transition from City Attorney (defending the City/LAPD) to Lead Plaintiff Counsel suing the City.
* **Hub:** Visualizing the shared physical location of the LA Alliance org, SLU Law, and California Alliance.
* **Money:** Documenting the specific court orders that funneled taxpayer funds into this legal network.
* **Timeline:** A chronological view of the case milestones contrasted with the tragic fate of the plaintiffs.

### 🛠 Tech Stack
* **D3.js:** For the force-directed graph simulation and timeline scaling.
* **Tailwind CSS:** For the mobile-responsive "Drawer" UI and dark-mode aesthetic.
* **HTML5/JavaScript:** For a zero-dependency, single-file deployment.

### 📲 How to View
1.  **Desktop:** Hover over nodes to see connections; click for full source citations.
2.  **Mobile:** Tap nodes to open the Detail Drawer. Switch to **Timeline View** for a vertical chronological breakdown.
